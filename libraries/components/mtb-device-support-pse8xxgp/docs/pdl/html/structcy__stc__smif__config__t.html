◆ deselectDelay

uint32_t cy_stc_smif_config_t::deselectDelay

Specifies the minimum duration of SPI de-selection between SPI transfers:

  • "0": 1 clock cycle.
  • "1": 2 clock cycles.
  • "2": 3 clock cycles.
  • "3": 4 clock cycles.
  • "4": 5 clock cycles.
  • "5": 6 clock cycles.
  • "6": 7 clock cycles.
  • "7": 8 clock cycles.
